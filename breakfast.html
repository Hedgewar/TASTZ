Idli

    
Ingredients

    1 cup regular rice
1 cup parboiled rice or 2 cups idli rice or 2 cups parboiled rice
1/2 cup urad dal (whole or split urad dal, 120 grams whole or split, husked black gram)
1/4 cup thick poha (20 grams, flattened rice)
1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (methi seeds)
2 cups water (for soaking rice)
1 cup water (for soaking urad dal)
1/2 cup water (for grinding urad dal or add as required)
1 cup water (for grinding rice or add as required)
1 teaspoon sea salt (or rock salt, edible and food grade)
Oil (as required to apply to the idli moulds)
2 1/2 cups water (for steaming idli)


Directions

Here are the steps to prepare the batter:
Rinse the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately and soak them in water for 4-5 hours.
Drain the water from the soaked urad dal and grind it to a smooth paste using 1/2 cup of water.
Drain the water from the soaked rice and grind it to a smooth paste using 1 cup of water.
Mix the urad dal and rice batter together in a large bowl.
Add salt to the batter and mix well.
Cover the bowl and let the batter ferment for 8-9 hours.
Grease the idli moulds with oil and pour the batter into the moulds.
Steam the idlis for 10-12 minutes on medium heat.
Serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney.
