Sandwich

    
Ingredients:

6 slices of bread (toasted and buttered)
4 tablespoons of mayonnaise
4 ounces of turkey (sliced)
4 ounces of ham (sliced)
2 slices of cheddar cheese
4 slices of bacon (cooked)
1 tomato (sliced)
1/2 cup of lettuce (washed and chopped)

Instructions:

Toast the bread slices and spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice.
On one slice of bread, place a slice of cheddar cheese, followed by turkey, ham, bacon, tomato, and lettuce.
Place another slice of bread on top of the lettuce and repeat the layering process.
Top with the remaining slice of bread and secure the sandwich with toothpicks.
Cut the sandwich into quarters and serve.

Preperation Time:

10 minutes
